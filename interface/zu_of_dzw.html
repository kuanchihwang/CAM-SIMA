private pure function zu_of_dzw_real32(dzw) result(zu)

Compute the coordinates at u-wind levels zu from the differences in between w-wind levels dzw, where is the vertical coordinate, u-wind and w-wind levels are synonymous with layer midpoints and interfaces in MPAS, respectively. (KCW, 2025-10-20)
