private pure function zw_of_dzw_real32(dzw) result(zw)

Compute the coordinates at w-wind levels zw from the differences in between w-wind levels dzw, where is the vertical coordinate, and w-wind levels are synonymous with layer interfaces in MPAS. (KCW, 2025-10-20)
